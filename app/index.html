I'm Sebastien Couture, General Partner at Interop, a venture capital fund backing teams building applications and protocols in the Interchain economy, know canonically as the Cosmos Network.

In 2013, I co-founded Epicenter, the best tech podcast covering the blockchain and cryptocurrency industry. Epicenter has been heard over 10 million times and produced over 400 episodes of in-depth, enlightening interviews with crypto founders and builders.

In 2015, I co-founded Stratumn, a VC-backed startup that provides document and process certification solutions to financial institutions.

A passionate cryptocurrency enthusiast, I co-founded and was communications Communications Director of Adan from 2020 to 2021, an industry association which promotes the development of the French cryptoasset and blockchain industry.

In the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, I organized Reset Everything, one of the first online conferences re-thinking old paradigms in times of crisis.

Born and raised in Canada, I'm a proud Acadian and have been living in France for over 15 years. Occasionally, I write and posts photography on my blog.
